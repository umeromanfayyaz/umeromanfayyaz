Current Research Projects

Board Composition and Innovative Start-ups

This project examines board composition, governance structures, and the role of female founders and directors in innovative start-ups, with particular attention to entrepreneurial outcomes and sustainability-oriented governance.

Corporate Governance and ESG Disclosure

This research focuses on how governance mechanisms, board diversity, and leadership composition influence ESG disclosure and sustainability practices across firms.

Female Leadership and Firm Performance

This project investigates the relationship between female participation in leadership roles and firm-level outcomes, including performance, innovation, and strategic decision-making.
